We develop Ranking Facts tool that aims to provide explanations about score-based rankings. Ranking Facts generates “nutritional label” that explains the ranking process and ranked outcomes to a user with appropriately summarized information. Specifically, the nutritional label in Ranking Facts is made up of a collection of visual widgets, where each widget shows an aspect of the outcomes such as fairness, diversity, a “nutritional label” for score-based rankings. Ranking Facts is made up of a collection of visual widgets, each showing an aspect of ranked outcomes including fairness, diversity, stability, and transparency. The fairness widget integrates the fairness measure rND proposed in the paper of Measuring fairness in ranked outputs and FA*IR proposed by Zehlike et al.

Ranking Facts can be accessed at dataresponsibly.github.io/tools/ Source code is available at GitHub.
