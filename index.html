My name is Ke Yang (杨珂). I’m a fifth-year PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Tandon School of Engineering, New York University. My research focuses on responsible data management and non-discriminatory machine learning. Specifically my research focuses on the multiple quality aspects of algorithmic outcomes: fairness, diversity, stability, etc, with the goal to develop methods to efficiently evaluate and improve it.


I’m a member of ViDA lab and my advisor is Prof. Julia Stoyanovich.
